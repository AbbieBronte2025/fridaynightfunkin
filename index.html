Game Description

Friday Night Funkin' is an exciting rhythm-based game where you'll face off in epic dance battles to impress your love interest, Boyfriend's girlfriend, and take on various quirky characters. The game combines catchy music, fun gameplay, and a vibrant 2D art style that keeps players coming back for more. Whether you're a rhythm game pro or a casual gamer, Friday Night Funkin' offers an engaging and challenging experience!

How to Play

Beat the Rhythm

Press the correct arrow keys in sync with the music to match the rhythm and outdance your opponent.

Challenge Your Friends

Compete against colorful characters, each with unique music tracks and dance moves, as you aim to win over your girlfriend's heart.

Unlock New Levels

As you progress through the game, unlock exciting new tracks and characters, and take on more difficult challenges.

Simple Controls, Big Fun

Use the arrow keys to match the beat, but don't miss a note! The better you perform, the more you impress your opponents.

Controls

WASD or arrow keys to play
+ and - to increase/decrease the volume
0 to mute
Enter to select
ESC to go back
